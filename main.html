Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is famously known as "The City of Light". It is renowned for its rich culture, iconic landmarks, world-class art museums, and exquisite cuisine. Visitors are enchanted by its romantic ambiance, historic architecture, and vibrant lifestyle.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic symbol of Paris offering breathtaking city views.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the Mona Lisa and countless masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Montmartre – Famous for its artistic vibe and Sacré-Cœur Basilica.
  5. Seine River Cruise – Enjoy a scenic journey along the Seine at night.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Baguettes – Traditional French bread with a crisp crust.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Escargot – A delicacy of garlic butter snails.
  • Macarons – Colorful, delicate almond cookies filled with ganache.
