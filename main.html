Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is renowned for its rich culture, stunning architecture, and romantic ambiance. It's a perfect destination for art lovers, history enthusiasts, and foodies alike.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – Iconic landmark offering panoramic views of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the world-famous Mona Lisa painting.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Montmartre – A historic district known for its artistic vibe and Sacré-Cœur Basilica.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A scenic boat ride showcasing Paris’ landmarks.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Escargot – A classic French delicacy made from snails.
  • Ratatouille – A traditional vegetable stew from Provence.
  • Crème Brûlée – Creamy dessert with a caramelized sugar topping.
  • Baguette – Freshly baked French bread, a staple in Paris.
